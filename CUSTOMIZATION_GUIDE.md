## üé® Design Tips

### Writing Effective Headlines

**Good:**
- "Lightning-fast fiber internet for your home"
- "Reliable broadband that won't let you down"
- "Stream, game, and work without interruptions"

**Avoid:**
- Too technical: "GPON-based FTTH with QoS"
- Too vague: "Good internet service"
- Too long: "We provide the best internet service in the entire region with..."

### Statistics Best Practices

**Use Round Numbers:**
- ‚úÖ "200+" not "187"
- ‚úÖ "99.9%" not "99.87%"
- ‚úÖ "24/7" not "23.5/7"

**Make Them Impressive:**
- ‚úÖ "5000+ Happy customers"
- ‚úÖ "10 Years in business"
- ‚úÖ "Zero downtime last month"

**Keep Labels Short:**
- ‚úÖ "Cities served" not "Number of cities we serve"
- ‚úÖ "Uptime" not "Network uptime percentage"

### Feature Descriptions

**Be Specific:**
- ‚úÖ "Up to 1 Gbps symmetrical speeds"
- ‚ùå "Very fast internet"

**Focus on Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ "Stream 4K on multiple devices simultaneously"
- ‚ùå "High bandwidth allocation"

**Keep It Short:**
- ‚úÖ 1-2 lines per feature
- ‚ùå Long paragraphs
